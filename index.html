About Moonfox

Moonfox is a modern divination companion built around the classical Chinese I Ching (六爻) tradition. Following the time-honored Zēngshān Bǔyì (增删卜易) method, it casts hexagrams just as ancient diviners did, then leverages large language models to translate the resulting symbols into plain, readable guidance.

Whether you are a long-time student of Yijing or someone simply curious about Eastern divination, Moonfox aims to be a calm, focused tool — no clutter, no gimmicks, just the question, the hexagram, and a thoughtful answer.

关于狐问六爻

狐问六爻 (Moonfox) 是一款现代化的占卜辅助工具， 围绕中国古典《周易》六爻传统打造，遵循《增删卜易》古法摇卦流程， 再借助大语言模型将卦象与爻辞翻译成清晰、易读的解读建议。

无论您是长期研习易学的爱好者，还是初次接触东方占卜的好奇者， 狐问六爻都希望成为一款安静、专注的小工具—— 没有冗余功能，没有炫技噱头，只有问题、卦象与一份认真的解读。
